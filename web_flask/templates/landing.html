About us

Our
history

We are Rodrigo Briones, Francisco Calixto and Gabriel Ferreira, developers of what is known today as ESPot. Having to come up with an idea for Holberton School end of first year portfolio project, we decided to dig into the emerging world of IoT and develop a self irrigating plant vase, which is able to communicate via wifi and exchange important data in order for the owner to keep up with his plant through our web page. Amongst all of the technologies used, the main character is the versatile ESP8266 microcontroller, the one able to connect to the wifi and send the data, as well as knowing when to irrigate with the help of a mini water pump, a soil moisture sensor and a water level sensor incorporated to a water tank.
Visit our project's github repository!

About us

Our
Goals

Our main objective as a team is to inspire and motivate people to have indoor plants. Eliminating any limiting factor at the moment of acquiring a plant, may it be the responsibility of having to take care of it or simply because of the time you have available to spend time on it. That is why ESPot is a unique product, adapted, automated and perfect for anyone thanks to its simple and intuitive web interface.
